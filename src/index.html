Food

We’ve reserved a pavilion with a charcoal grill, and we’ll be providing standard cookout stuff—Hot dogs, brats, burgers, and all their vegetarian equivalents. Snacks will also be provided. If you want something more fun, feel free to bring whatever you’d like to add to the grill and any other snacks or dessert. No one will be mad if you bring brownies.

Drinks

We’ll have several coolers full of non-alcoholic sparkling stuff and sodas. Our permit allows for 3.2% alcohol only, and no kegs, so we can’t get too sloppy. Bring your own beer on the sly or save the drinks for the after party.

The Park

Boom Island has lots of parking, a big ol’ pavilion reserved for us, lots of open green space for games, and a nice playground. A ton more details can be found on the city’s website.

Weather

Our pavilion should keep us out of the rain, so we plan on gathering rain or shine.
